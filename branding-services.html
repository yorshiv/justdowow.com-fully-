Common Questions

Branding FAQ

How much does branding cost in Ghaziabad?+
Cost depends on scope — a logo and basic visual identity costs less than a full brand strategy with guidelines and packaging design. Book a free brand consultation and we'll give you a clear quote based on your specific requirements.
What is the difference between a logo and a brand identity?+
A logo is one visual mark. A brand identity is the complete system — logo, colors, typography, imagery style, and tone of voice — that makes your business instantly recognisable across every touchpoint.
How long does a branding project take?+
A logo and basic identity typically takes 2-3 weeks. A full brand strategy with guidelines, packaging, and social media branding can take 4-6 weeks depending on scope and feedback turnaround.
Do you offer brand strategy or just design?+
Both. We start with brand strategy and market research before any visual design begins, so the identity we create is grounded in your positioning and audience, not just aesthetics.
Will I receive brand guidelines after the project?+
Yes. Every branding project includes a brand guidelines document covering logo usage, color codes, typography, and tone of voice, so your team or any future designer can stay consistent.
Can you rebrand an existing business?+
Yes. We offer full rebranding services that modernise your visual identity while preserving the brand equity and recognition you've already built.
Do you design packaging and social media branding too?+
Yes. Beyond logo and core identity, we design packaging, social media templates, and marketing creatives so your brand looks consistent everywhere it appears.
Is branding different from marketing?+
Branding is who you are — your identity, positioning, and visual system. Marketing is how you communicate that to the world through channels like SEO, social media, and ads. Strong branding makes every marketing effort more effective.
Do you offer branding for personal brands, not just companies?+
Yes. We offer personal branding services for founders, consultants, and professionals who want a consistent, credible presence across their website and social platforms.
Do you offer branding services outside Ghaziabad?+
Yes. While we have deep local expertise in Ghaziabad, Tronica City and Indirapuram, we provide branding services for clients across Delhi NCR and remotely throughout India.
